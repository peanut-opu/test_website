計畫簡介

本網站展示黑潮在台灣東方海域的流速特性，並探討其作為海洋能發電的潛力。 內容包含歷史時間序列分析和黑潮平均流場空間分布（動態）。 This website presents the characteristics of the Kuroshio Current speed east of Taiwan and explores its potential for ocean energy power generation. The content includes historical time-series analysis and an animated spatial distribution of the mean Kuroshio flow field.
